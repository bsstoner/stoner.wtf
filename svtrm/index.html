Urban Sophistication

Perfect for the active, effortlessly put-together mavericks-on-the-go. You will be traveling with truely one of kind set of accessories.

This is a limited edition collection of handmade bags in 4 different sizes and styles.

100% Cotton Canvas Bags

The bags are 100% waxed cotton canvas providing a much more durable and long-lasting bag than typical canvas backpacks.

The collection was designed by SKINNY VINNY a Brooklyn based designer that specializing in designing and producing high quality bags for the past 10 years.

Upcycled Leather

The leather trim is made using the scraps from production runs at Prachi Leathers, in Kanpur, India. The individual pieces of leather are sewn together to create a unique blend of color shades, textures and grains giving each bag a personality all it's own.

The manufacturing process was lead by TRM TAB a maker of upcycled leather goods.
